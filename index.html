Guys and Gals Bass Club of Northwest Florida

Established in 2004, the Guys and Gals Bass Club of Northwest Florida is for couples who enjoy fishing together, an outlet, or an opportunity to meet and fish with other like minded couples. We compete in fun, tournament style fishing, but most importantly we have a good time.
